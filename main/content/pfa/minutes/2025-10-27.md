### 4. Chair’s Report - JS

It has been a busy fundraising year.

- Trim Trail successfully installed – a great asset to both the school and the wider
  community - available after school hours.
- Fundraising activities included: Sponsored walk, Bake sale, and stall at Ayton Flower Show.
- Fraser McGregor will provide a quote for improving the wood chip area (woodchip
  removed and fencing and composite non slip decking installed).
- JS noted that weekly check-ins with the school at Breakfast Club have been useful.
- JS is happy to continue as Chair but open to swapping with a Vice Chair since her child is in P7.

### 7. Election of Office Bearers

- Secretary: Katie Aitchison
- Chair: Joanna Stewart
- Vice-Chair: Laura Cooksey
- Treasurer: Toby Foster

It was agreed to edit a line to the constitution allowing the Chair to remain in post if their
child is in P7, provided a Vice Chair is in place. (TF, JS, LB, KA, LC, RvK)

Current wording “The Chair should not be a parent that has only a P7 pupil in school. This is
to ensure effective handover and AGM attendance, for the following academic year.”

Proposed change “At least one of the Chair or Vice Chair should expect to still be a parent of
a child at the school in the following academic year. This is to ensure effective handover and
AGM attendance.”

Agreed - LB, KA
